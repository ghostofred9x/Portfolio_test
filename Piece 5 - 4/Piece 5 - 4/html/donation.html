Donations

Catholics

United for the Faith is a lay apostolate founded in 1968 to support, defend, and advance the efforts of the teaching Church.

Our founder, H. Lyman Stebbins, said that CUF exists “to be a little alarm clock to wake people up, and then a center around which they can rally, and act in the way befitting members of Christ’s true Church.”

CUF’s sole purpose is to help build up the Church and her members. When you support CUF, you help ensure that the Catholic faith is conveyed accurately and authentically.

How can I support CUF and its mission?


You can support CUF in a variety of ways (click each link to learn more):
  • Pray for our work. According to our founder, H. Lyman Stebbins, “Study, prayer, fasting, and works of mercy and love toward our neighbor . . . are, necessarily and objectively, prerequisites for the effectiveness of any Christian work.”
  • Become a member or renew your membership. For an annual tax-deductible contribution of $40 or more, members of CUF support CUF's apostolic endeavors and enjoy many member benefits.
  • Sign up as a monthly donor. The generous contributions of our monthly donors help CUF's daily operations to run smoothly and efficiently.
  • Make a pledge to our capital campaign. As CUF approaches its fortieth anniversary, we are engaged in an unprecedented initiative to expand and improve the CUF apostolate. Click here to read more.
  • Provide for the future of CUF through planned giving. Ensure that CUF will be able to carry out its mission both now and in the years to come.
  • Give a gift of stock.
  • Make a one-time donation.
  • Join or start a local CUF chapter.

We value each and every contributor.
